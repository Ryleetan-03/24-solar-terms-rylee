Beginning of Spring

‘Start of Spring’ marks the end of winter and the beginning of the spring. The weather becomes noticeably warmer, and plants start to grow again. Due to China's vast territory, spring comes at a different times from north to south. ‘Start of Spring’ is only a prelude to spring for many northern regions. Everything has not yet left its frozen state in most of northern China around February 4th. The Spring Festival (Chinese New Year) marks the first day of the Chinese lunar calendar. Because it is regulated to occur within half a lunar month of ‘Start of Spring’, people call it the Spring Festival and it welcomes the coming spring. It is the first and the most important festival in the Chinese calendar.

Spring Equinox

The Spring Equinox is the fourth solar term of spring. It is crossed every year on March 19-22 of the Gregorian calendar. The spring equinox has an important significance in astronomy, the day and night of the northern and southern hemispheres are equally divided, since this day after the direct position of the sun continues to push from the equator to the northern hemisphere, the northern hemisphere around the day began to longer than the night, the southern hemisphere and the opposite. In terms of climate, there are also more obvious features, China except for the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau, northeast, northwest and northern China have entered a bright spring

Fresh Green

Qing Ming is an ancient festival of the Chinese people. It is a solemn festival for sweeping tombs and paying respects to ancestors, but also a joyful festival for people to get close to nature and enjoy the fun of spring. At this time of the year, the sun is shining, the grass is budding, flowers are blooming, the air is clear and bright, and all things are visible, and nature presents a vibrant scene. In the southern part of China, the climate is refreshingly warm, and the land shows the sign of spring and brightness. In the northern regions, the snow has also started to break, and gradually enter the sunny spring; the temperature in the northern regions rises quickly at this time of the year, precipitation is scarce, dry and windy, and it is the time of the year when there is a lot of dusty weather.
